# 1. INTRODUCTION & ORIGIN

GPU-45 is one of the most widely adopted Finger Millet varieties developed by the University of Agricultural Sciences, Bengaluru. It is known for high grain yield, excellent adaptability, strong blast tolerance, and superior grain quality. GPU-45 became popular among farmers due to stable production under both rainfed and irrigated conditions.
