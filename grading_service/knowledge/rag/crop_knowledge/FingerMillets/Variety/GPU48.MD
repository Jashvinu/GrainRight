# 1. INTRODUCTION & ORIGIN

GPU 48 is a high-yielding Finger Millet variety developed by the PC Unit, University of Agricultural Sciences (UAS), Bengaluru. It was developed from the cross GPU 26 × L5 and released in 2005. GPU 48 is known for early maturity, blast resistance, and suitability for irrigated cultivation.
