# 1. INTRODUCTION & ORIGIN OF RICE

Rice (Oryza sativa) is one of the world's most important cereal crops and serves as the staple food for more than half of the global population. Rice has been cultivated for thousands of years and plays a major role in food security, nutrition, and agricultural economies worldwide.

India is one of the largest rice-producing countries and cultivates numerous varieties including Indrayani, Basmati, and Kolam.
