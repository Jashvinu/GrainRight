# 1. INTRODUCTION & ORIGIN OF BASMATI RICE

Basmati Rice is one of the world's most famous aromatic rice varieties. The name "Basmati" originates from Sanskrit, meaning "Fragrant" or "Queen of Fragrance." It is known for its extra-long grain length, distinctive aroma, fluffy texture after cooking, and premium export value. Basmati rice is cultivated primarily in India and Pakistan and is highly demanded in international markets.
