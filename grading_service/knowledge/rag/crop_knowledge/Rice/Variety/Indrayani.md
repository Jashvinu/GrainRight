# 1. INTRODUCTION & ORIGIN OF INDRAYANI RICE

Indrayani Rice is one of Maharashtra's most popular aromatic rice varieties. It is widely cultivated in Western Maharashtra and selected regions of North Maharashtra due to its excellent cooking quality, pleasant aroma, soft texture and strong consumer demand. The variety was developed for Maharashtra's agro-climatic conditions and has become a preferred rice variety among farmers and consumers.

## Scientific Classification

| Parameter       | Details              |
| --------------- | -------------------- |
| Common Name     | Indrayani Rice       |
| Scientific Name | Oryza sativa L.      |
| Family          | Poaceae              |
| Crop Type       | Aromatic Rice        |
| Grain Category  | Medium Grain Rice    |
| Origin          | Maharashtra, India   |
| Colour          | White to Cream White |
