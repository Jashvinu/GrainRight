# 1. INTRODUCTION & ORIGIN OF KOLAM RICE

Kolam Rice is one of Maharashtra's most popular medium-grain rice varieties. It is widely consumed across Maharashtra because of its soft texture, pleasant taste, affordability, and adaptability to different agro-climatic conditions. Kolam is commonly used for daily household consumption and commercial food preparation.
