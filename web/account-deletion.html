Delete your Kalsubai Farms account

Kalsubai Farms users can request deletion of their farmer or FPO/FPC account. Farmer deletion covers the login, profile, farm boundaries, survey answers, field photos, grading records, diagnostics, and synced farm records. FPO/FPC deletion covers the workspace login, organization profile, farmer verification scans, procurement records, grading records, and received harvest-lot records.

To request deletion, email the account phone number, farmer ID, or FPO/FPC account email from your registered account. We may contact you to verify ownership before permanent deletion.

Email deletion request

In the mobile app, the same request is available from Farmer Settings, under Privacy and support.
